    About Us

    Knox Climate Control is an affordable, efficient, and reliable HVAC company located in Central Florida to keep your family comfortable.

    Our Mission

    Knox Climate Control aims to provide quality design, installation and service for Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning (HVAC) equipment in both residential and light commercial markets.

    Our Plan

    Knox Climate Control is about treating you like we would our own friends and family. Each customer is a priority that we place out values and quality workmanship into.

    Our Values

    Provide 100% customer satisfaction. We will be honest with you during your estimate process and work with you throughout the process of designing your next HVAC install. Knox Climate Control will help look for ways to cut cost, not corners.

    Services

    Cooling

    From central air to mini split systems, we can install it all for better air quality and a better nights rest.

    Heating

    Get rid of the space heaters and the window units. Let us help you install or update that old heat pump system.

    Ventilation

    Need a boost of fresh air into the home? We can help you plan where an outdoor air exchanger can fit into your current system.

    Design

    Wether it's a new build or a new install, we can help you design, layout and plan for that HVAC overhaul.

    Going Green

    The average system lasts about 10-12 years. Why waste energy and money?! We can help you upgrade at an affordable price.

    Maintenance

    As with anything, maintenance is key. However your life is busy! So let Knox Climate Control help with routine maintenance. Ask us about our plans.
